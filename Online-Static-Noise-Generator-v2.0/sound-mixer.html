How to Mix:
• Use sliders to balance sound volumes.
• Click + Add Sound to load audio files.
• Tracks should be at least 10 seconds long to ensure that a 3-second crossfade is fully applied. This will prevent clicking during audio loops.
• Click Export to render the mix into a high-quality WAV.
• Note: Streaming online is infinite, but file exports are capped at 2 hours to prevent system memory limit errors. Downloading long duration audio files may fail on mobile devices.
